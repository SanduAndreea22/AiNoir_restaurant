About Al Noir

At Al Noir, every meal is a celebration of the tradition and authentic flavors of the Middle East.

Restaurant History

Al Noir was founded with the desire to bring authentic Arab tradition to the hearts of those who appreciate the culinary culture of the Middle East. Since our opening, we have aimed to be more than just a restaurant – a place where tastes and aromas create a unique atmosphere, and every guest feels part of a one-of-a-kind experience.

Each recipe we serve is inspired by the ancient traditions of Arab cuisine, brought to life with the finest ingredients and a passion for detail.

Our Vision

Al Noir’s vision is to be a reference point for lovers of Arab cuisine, offering our guests an authentic culinary experience in an elegant and comfortable setting. We aim to pay tribute to Arab traditions, drawing inspiration from the cultural and gastronomic diversity of the region to create dishes that tell an unforgettable story.

Our Mission

Al Noir’s mission is to offer our customers a flawless culinary experience that blends tradition with modernity, respecting authentic recipes while adding a touch of innovation. We strive to become a place where every guest feels special, and each meal is an opportunity to explore and understand Arab culture through our dishes.

Inspired by Arab Tradition

Each dish at Al Noir is inspired by the richness and diversity of Arab traditions. We use recipes passed down through generations, adapted to reflect the finest tastes of the present. From savory dishes like shawarma and falafel to our delicious desserts, everything is prepared with respect for tradition and attention to detail.

Additionally, the restaurant’s atmosphere, elegant decor, and our attention to detail contribute to creating a unique experience where Arab tradition blends seamlessly with modern comfort.
